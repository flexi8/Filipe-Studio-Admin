🎧 Music Experience

Each track is stored in the cloud and streamed directly from Firebase — meaning faster load, cleaner sound, and no dead links. You can click any song, get the name, watch the custom animated progress bar move, and even see what’s up next.

And the best part? Auto-Play Next keeps the fire going without you lifting a finger.

🛒 Merch & Drops

Looking fresh? You should be. The Merch section links you straight to the latest drops — hoodies, tees, caps, and maybe even something special from Filipes himself.

📹 Videos & Events

Every artist and label needs visuals. Filipe Studio features video links to YouTube, premieres, and interviews. The Events tab keeps it all organized — whether it's a street gig, studio launch, or listening party.

🔒 Real-Time & Secure

Built on Google Firebase, every upload is synced in real-time. That means no page refresh needed. Whether it's new music, merch, or event info, everything flows smoothly and securely through cloud storage and Firestore DB.
